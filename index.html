What is the capital gains yield calculator?
The capital gains yield calculator is a tool that measures how much an investment has risen due to the increase in price which is useful when analyzing your investment returns.
How to calculate capital gains yield?
To calculate capital gains yield you should use the following formula:
capital gains yield = ((current price - bought price) / bought price) * 100
Why use capital gains yield calculator?
The capital gains yield calculator is used in order to determine how much an investment has risen due to the increase in price.
